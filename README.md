## Client Brief

*Client:* Niclas Grosch — a gym enthusiast who trains regularly and wants a more structured, personalized way to plan his workouts and track progress.

*Purpose:* To create an interactive quiz-style website that generates a complete, customized gym plan based on a few guided inputs. 

*Audience:* Individuals who go to the gym and want a clear, structured weekly workout plan tailored to their goals, experience level, and schedule.

*Key action:* Complete the quiz and generate a personalized weekly workout plan (with optional PDF download).

*Pages/sections needed:*
•⁠ ⁠Landing page with brief explanation and “Build My Plan” button
•⁠ ⁠Interactive quiz flow (fitness goal, experience level, days per week, workout duration, target muscle groups, equipment access)
•⁠ ⁠Workout split reveal section (e.g., Push/Pull/Legs, Upper/Lower, Full Body)
•⁠ ⁠Weekly schedule overview section
•⁠ ⁠Exercise breakdown section (sets, reps, rest time)
•⁠ ⁠Progress tracker section
•⁠ ⁠Download page / PDF export

*Content status:* Core quiz structure and workout templates are planned. Still need final branding direction from the client (app name, preferred aesthetic, motivational tone, and whether it will remain personal or expand publicly).


*Style preferences:* Clean and minimalist design with a strong, athletic feel. Dark mode with bold typography and subtle accent colors for a modern gym-inspired aesthetic.

*Inspiration sites:*
•⁠ ⁠Nike Training Club — liked the clean structure and professional workout layout
•⁠ ⁠Strong App — liked the simple logging system and minimal interface
